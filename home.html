Welcome to

Parokya sa Sagrada Familia Laak Music Ministry

At parokya Sagrada Familia-Laak, we believe in the power of music to uplift spirits and bring communities together in worship. Our mission is to serve the Lord God and His church through music the gift of music. This website serves as hub for our choir members, parishioners, and visitors to connect, engage, and participate in our vibrant musical community. Explore our resources, stay updated on events, and discover how you can get involved in our ministry.

Our Mission and Vision

The PSF-Laak Music Ministry is dedicated to enhancing the liturgical experience through music. Our Vision is to create an ecouraging platform that hightlights our commitment to serving God and the church. We aim foster a welcoming environment for all who wish to join us.
